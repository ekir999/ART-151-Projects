Rap/Hip-Hop was introducted as a music genre during the 1970's in Brooklyn, NY in which block parties would be hosted and DJ's started implementing beats sometimes with soul, funk and disco genres aswell.

Some of the notable hip-hop artists during the 1970's were DJ Kool Herc and Coke La Rock. They were some of the first to host the block parties in which they were similar to parties .
