During the 2010's, many artists started becoming independent, creating their own brands and fanbases along with the rise and fall of SoundCloud along with the rise of Spotify, two of the most popular music platforms in which people listen to music on the go. During this decade, more subgenres came to be invented such as emo rap, "SoundCloud rap"or mumble rap along with revival of gangsta rap with subgenres such as drill rap from Chicago, New York, UK, and other places across the world.

Some notable artists during 2010's hip-hop period were A$AP Mob, Tyler the Creator, Migos, J. Cole, Logic and many other artists.
