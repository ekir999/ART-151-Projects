During the 1980's the hip-hop genre started gaining some traction when hip-hop groups such as the N.W.A became mainstream along with the gangsta and g-funk subgenres of rap/hip-hop .

Some notable artists during 1980's hip-hop period were the N.W.A, Run-D.M.Z, Public Enemy and LL Cool J.
