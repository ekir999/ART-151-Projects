Next Introduction to the Rap/Hip-Hop Music Genre

Rap/Hip-Hop was introducted as a music genre during the 1970's in Brooklyn, NY in which block parties would be hosted and DJ's started implementing beats sometimes with soul, funk and disco genres aswell.

There are many instruments used to create rap/hip-hop songs, albums and mixtapes but the most notable one is the Roland's TR-808 drum machine used to create drum beats digitally and 808 beats along with DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) programs such Ableton Live and FL Studio.
